    Hallmark's Bespoke exp



    This is the first projects that contains all essential elements that are required to create from a problem statement a full blown design solution that moslty secures the user's and business's needs.

    Not only did we need to perform all stages of the design process, but we needed to perform several iterations of reached results in order to excel and develop a truly wonderful experience for our user and pontentially many more, if Hallmark approves of the design solution.

    The three main parts of the experiecne - Crowdfunding, Interactive card & Digital unwrapping were constraint within the individual's (we had a special research subject) interest and hobbies.

    All interactive elements within the experience are based on insights that were derived from anylzing existing and previously made research with the subject himself.

    The video to the right, briefly shows the gift exchange we made earlier, just so we could have some level of pre-final design testing to see if we're in the right direction.

    The final Expo where we are to present the design solution is coming up and with a scalable, cost-effective solution that combines what we analized into something engaging, meaningful and relateable, we have high confidence.
